About
Fur Friends.

A cold, rainy night in Burlington, VT

We remember it like it was yesterday. Picture four college roommates in 1999 on a typical gloomy November evening heading out for dinner. Standing on the front porch of our apartment house, we were ready to go grab some pizza.

Little did we know, the resilient cat that would inspire our animal rescue mission had taken refuge just beneath our feet.

It started with a meow

After investigating a faint meow, we found the calico who would eventually be known as Sadie. Sadie had taken shelter under our porch and was suffering from injury. We decided to skip the pizza and take her to be treated. We discovered her tail and leg were crushed by a car and after needing to amputate both she was in for a long recovery.

And the rest is history

Sadie made a full recovery and eventually went to live with Clarisa after graduation, where she thrived until crossing the Rainbow Bridge in 2007. Sadie inspired us to start an organization dedicated to animal welfare and rescue. Her legacy remains strong in our mission at Fur Friends as a reminder that all animals deserve a happy life.
